Teamworking

Good team

  • Psychological safety.
  • Dependability & mutual accountability.
  • Structure & Clarity
  • The work has personal meaning.
  • The work has outward impact.

Hierarchy of Awareness

For a team, the sequence is

Task > Structure > Roles > Behaviours > Emotions.

This means, a team gets a task. The task woud naturally generate a structure (task could be separated into sub-tasks). These sub-tasks generates different roles, which gets assigned into different people, generating different emotions.

For individuals, the sequence is reversed.

Emotions > Behaviours > Roles > Structure > Task.

Presentations

Presentation = One persion speaks to others with a purpose.

Presentation needs attentions of others, which is very expensive (as a way to communicate).

Fear of presentation = fear of humiliated

Preparation

Typically, I need to practice the content, my voice and my body language.

These tricks helps with the voice.

  • Chew gum helps the voice (warm up the mouse muscles, reduce nerversness)
  • Say every Word
  • Finish your thought (before starting next one) → Pause a lot, don’t rush
